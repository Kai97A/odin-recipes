pasta bolognese

pasta bolognese recipe

this is how to make some good pasta bolognese

you can also modify the recipe for your own taste!

ingrediens

  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 diced carrot
  • 3dl creamy tomato-herbs sauce
  • 1/4 ts salt

steps

  1. Fry the minced meat with onion and carrot in a pan. If necessary, use oil for frying.
  2. Add the sauce to the pan and season with salt. Heat up.
  3. Prepare the pasta according to the instructions on the package. Serve pasta with bolognese sauce.
