chicken stew recipe

this is how to make some good chicken stew

you can also modify the recipe for your own taste!

ingrediens

  • 400g thin chicken fillet steaks
  • 1/4 tea spoon and blackpepper
  • 100q chorizo-sausage
  • 1/2 dl jam onions
  • 150g zucchini
  • 1dl applejuice
  • 3dl creamy tomato-herbs sauce
  • 1 table spoon wine vinegar
  • 1/2 dl fresh basil
  • 1/2 dl salted almonds

steps

  1. Season the halved fillets with pepper and salt. Dice the chorizo ​​into small pieces. Cut the washed zucchini into pieces.
  2. Fry the pieces of chorizo ​​in a pan or a frying pan, enough so that the fat comes off on the pan. Add the chicken pieces and fry until they have a frying surface.
  3. Add zucchini, jam onions or olives, apple juice and sauce. Rinse the sauce jar with a splash of water and pour it in.
  4. Heat, stirring, until the sauce boils. Cover with a lid and simmer under the lid for 15 min.
  5. Season with vinegar. Finish the stew with basil and crushed salted almonds.
  6. Serve chicken stew with rice, country bread or couscous (note: bread and couscous contain gluten).
