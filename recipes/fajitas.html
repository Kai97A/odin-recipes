fajitas recipe

this is how to make some good fajitas

you can also modify the recipe for your own taste!

ingrediens

  • 1-2ts butter
  • 400g unseasoned chiken strip
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carlic cloves
  • 1 yellow paprika
  • 1 green paprika
  • 1 bag of fajitas seasons
  • 2dl food cream

steps

  1. Brown the chicken strips in butter. Add chopped onion and garlic and chopped peppers. Cook for a while. Add the spice mixture and cream. Stir and heat.
  2. Heat the tortillas according to the instructions on the package. Add salad, chicken filling, salsa sauce, crème fraîche and cheese to the tortillas.
